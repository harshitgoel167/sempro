WHAT WE DO

We build a better future for America’s youth by connecting
needy ones to the real heroes in our society.they want to share to help others .

Our mission is to break the cycle of poverty, illiteracy and low expectations through service and education. We empower U.S. youth
to transform their neighborhoods through intensive community service and to change the world by building schools in some of the
                                                                                   economically poorest countries on the planet.
